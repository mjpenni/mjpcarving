Church Sign
1969-ChurchSign.jpg - This carving was in a log probably 3 feet wide and 10 or 12 feet long. The sign was in place and carved. The pastor had just changed, so my job was to remove the previous pastor's name and replace it with the new pastor’s name. I did not do the whole sign. I was still in high school at the time and working at Lenz Arts.
Lenz Arts sign
1970-LenzArts.jpg - Lenz Arts sign. Lenz Arts moved into a building at the north end of North Pacific Avenue. The sign was made of clear heart redwood and as of 2024 the sign is still on the Lenz Arts building. Due to the size of the logo and the sign a chain saw was used to achieve the general shape.
Lenz Arts plaque
1970-LenzArts3.jpg - Lenz Arts plaque. Lenz Arts logo was an “L” in the form of a bent paint tube. The plaque was executed in Philippine mahogany. The image was executed as a negative, that is rather than being a bas-relief the design is incised into the wood.
